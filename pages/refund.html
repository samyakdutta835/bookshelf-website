Refund Policy

Bookshelf provides a simple and customer-friendly refund process to ensure a smooth shopping experience.

Eligible Refunds

Refunds are available for damaged, defective, or incorrect books delivered to customers.



Refund Request Time

Customers can request a refund within 7 days after receiving the order.



Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method.



Contact Support

For refund assistance, contact our support team through the Contact page.
